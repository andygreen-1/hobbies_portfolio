michiblogs was a collection of blogs and other creative content produced by students at the University of Michigan. Founded in the summer of 2012, michiblogs grew to have about 30 blogs and over 100,000 page views.

The Michigan Daily wrote an article about the site, which can be found [here](https://www.michigandaily.com/uncategorized/website-launched-student-bloggers/).
